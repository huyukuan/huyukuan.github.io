Biography

Yukuan Hu is a Ph.D. candidate of Optimization Group at Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), under the supervision of Professor Xin Liu. He received the Bachelor degree (2019) in mathematics and applied mathematics at Tongji University. His current research interests focus on developing efficient numerical optimization methods with theoretical guarantees for scientific and engineering problems, especially those arising in computational materials science.
